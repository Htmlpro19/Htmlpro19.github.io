About Me

Hi, my name is Dani Amir, and I'm a recent graduate from the University of Texas at Austin with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Entrepreneurship. I'm originally from Dallas, TX, and I got my first taste of programming when I was in high school, learning how to make games using Roblox!

I have experience in gameplay programming, AI programming, and XR development. I'm experienced in several different game engines and programming languages, including Unreal Engine 4/5, Unity, C++, C#, Python, and Lua. Outside of game development, I've had some exposure to frontend development using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and React.

Some of my favorite games are Skyrim, Halo 3, The Forest, Call of Duty Black Ops, and Star Wars Battlefront 2.
